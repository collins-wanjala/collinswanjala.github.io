AWS Free Tier Account Creation
Managed to create a free AWS free tier account.
An AWS Free Tier account offers new users the opportunity to explore Amazon Web Services at no cost for 12 months, providing access to popular services like EC2, S3, RDS, and Lambda with free usage limits. This is ideal for learning, testing, and building small applications without incurring charges. To create a free tier AWS account, visit aws.amazon.com/free, click “Create a Free Account,” and follow the steps to sign up using your email, set a password, provide billing information (a credit/debit card is required for identity verification), and select the basic support plan. Once activated, you can start deploying cloud resources and monitor usage through the AWS Management Console..
